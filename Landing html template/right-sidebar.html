ASL

American Sign Language (ASL) is being used as predominant sign language in USA, Canada, Mexico and 20 other countries. ASL is a natural language originated in the early 19th century in the American School for the Deaf in West Hartford, Connecticut. It's estimated that approximately about 500,000 childeren and adults are currenly using ASL as their primary language. We believe that Sign recognition in computer vision reserch would be very interesting and benifical for ASL users in many ways.
